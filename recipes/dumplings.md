# Dumplings

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity |
| --- | --- |
| Flour | 1 cup |
| Baking powder | 2 tsp |
| Sugar | 1 tsp |
| Salt | $\frac{1}{2}$ tsp |
| Butter | 1 Tbsp |
| Milk | $\frac{1}{2}$ cup |

## Directions

1. Drop into simmering stew/soup. Cover for 15 minutes.

2. Uncover and move pot off burner for another 15 minutes.
